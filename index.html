who we are

Rovers football club is a competitive and social football club based on Guam.Rovers FC participate in all league competitions of the guam football association the island wide football competition. Rovers FC have men's and women's and youth teams in Guam premier division, as well as recreational, master and youth program that includes U6, U8, U10, U12 and U15 youth divisions.
